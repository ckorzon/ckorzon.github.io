About Me

Hi! My name is Conrad, welcome to my site. I'm currently an undergraduate Computer Science major at the University of Connecticut. Moving forward, I plan to focus on software development, but I have an interest an all areas of Computer Science and as such I currently work part time as a Student Web Developer at UConn's University Communications office. I love both my classes and my work, as even though it has all kept me quite busy, I've learned more in the past few years than I had ever imagined I would. Here's a list of some of the topics I've worked with:

  • Object oriented programming
  • Python
  • Recursion
  • HTML, CSS, SCSS
  • Javascript ('vanilla', Angular, jQuery)
  • Runtime analysis
  • Algorithms

Some fun facts:

I was born in Connecticut and have lived here my whole life. I graduated from Coginchaug Regional Highschool, in Durham CT, in 2017. Our towns, Middlefield and Durham, are so small that our graduating class only had about 140 students. My first experience in programming was during my junior year at Coginchaug when the school began offering an introductory class in Python. After completing the course I went on to help teach it the following semester, which is when I decided on Computer Science as my major.
